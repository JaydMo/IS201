Experience

Here is what I have done.

Life is not just to be lived, it is to be experienced. Here is my experience:

  • Beyond earning my BSA Eagle Scout Award, I earned all Merit Badges and Palms in the program
    When I was 12 years old, I started Boy Scouts and set the goal to achieve every Merit Badge Award that existed. I worked diligently, coordinated meetings and activities with hundreds of people, and worked steadily to achieve the result I desired. I also achieved my goal of earning my Eagle Scout Rank and all the Eagle Palms. One skill I gained from this came from the Cinematography and Photography Merit Badges where I learned the fundamentals of camera usage.

  • I completed a two year mission for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints
    I was a full-time proselyting missionary in Northern California from 2019-2021. I learned how to deal with difficult situations, work diligently, and solve problems. I worked on a team that helped run and manage the social media page of our mission. I was also responsible for several reports using Google Suite Tools, and I was responsible for housing and transportation logistics for over 200 missionaries.

  • I am an accomplished classical pianist
    I have placed in dozens of piano competitions in Utah and Salt Lake Counties. I was a Guest Performing Artist with the Timpanogos Symphony Orchestra in the season finale concert and with the Utah Baroque Ensemble in 2017. I am familiar with the concert and performance world.
